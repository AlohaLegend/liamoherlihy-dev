How this works

A website build, explained like a normal project.

  1. Send the messy version. The old site, the new idea, the notes app version, or just what the business does.
  2. I find the confusing parts. The first impression, mobile path, missing proof, contact flow, and anything that makes people hesitate.
  3. I build the clear version. Pages, words, images, buttons, forms, launch, and the boring parts that make the site actually work.
  4. You get the keys. The live site, access, notes, and a plain-English explanation of what changed.
