Notes

The process

A calm way to rebuild a website.

  1. Send the rough notes. The old site, the new idea, the notes app dump, or just what the business does.
  2. I find the confusing parts. The first impression, mobile path, missing trust, contact flow, and anything that makes people hesitate.
  3. I build the useful site. Pages, words, images, buttons, forms, launch, and the boring parts that make the site actually work.
  4. You get the keys. The live site, access, notes, and a simple explanation of what changed.
